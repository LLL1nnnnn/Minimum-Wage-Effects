In the following experiment you have the opportunity to earn money depending on your behavior. Please turn off your mobile phone and do not talk to other participants in the experiment. It is very important that you follow these rules. If you have any question while reading these instructions or during the experiment itself, we ask you to raise your hand. We will immediately answer your question individually.

During the experiment you have the opportunity to do a task. The task consists of encoding combinations of letters (words) into numbers. In the task, fifteen capital letters always yield a word. You have to allocate a number to each capital letter. The encryption code can be found in a table below the corresponding letter. The computer program randomly selects in the table a new set of three-digit numbers to be used for the encoding of the capital letters. Additionally, the computer program shuffles the position of the capital letters in the table. Please note that the program always uses all 26 capital letters of the German alphabet.

To make an input, please click on the text box below the first capital letter. You can easily switch to the next box by using the tab key on your keyboard. You will have 60 seconds to encode as many letters as you can. The three-digit numbers in the encryption table are not selectable.

Then you will be randomly and anonymously matched with another participant, where one of you will be selected at random to be Employer; the other will be Worker. The Employer is hiring a Worker to complete the aforementioned encoding task by offer the Worker a wage.

Please click on the Next button if you are ready to see the task.
