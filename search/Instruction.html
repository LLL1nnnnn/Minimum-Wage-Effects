In the first task, you will play {{Constants.num_rounds}} rounds of the search game.

In each round, you are going to search for an item to purchase. You start with no item on your searched list. You can choose to search for the next item at a constant cost of {{ search_cost }} per search by clicking on Search button. For each item, there are two possible outcomes: {{ value_high }} and {{ value_low }}. The items differ in probability of getting {{ value_high }}. After each search, you will know the probability p of the item you just searched, where p is the probability of getting {{ value_high }}.

With the current searched list, you can choose to purchase any item by entering the Search No. of the item that you want to purchse in the box and clicking on the Purchase button; or you may keep searching for the next new item at cost of {{ search_cost }}. The round ends when you make a purchase.

{% if certainty == True %}

The value you receive from the item you choose to purchase is {{ value_high }} × p + {{ value_low }} × (1 - p).

Suppose you have searched n times in this round, your payoff in this round is - n × {{ search_cost }} + {{ value_high }} × p + {{ value_low }} × (1 - p).

{% if value_high == 500 %}

Example. Suppose you have searched 4 times and the item you choose to purchase has Probability of Being {{ value_high }} as 0.75 (p = 0.75), then your payoff is calculated as - 4 × {{ search_cost }} + {{ value_high }} × 0.75 + {{ value_low }} × (1 - 0.75) = 380.

{% elif value_high == 200 %}

Example. Suppose you have searched 4 times and the item you choose to purchase has Probability of Being {{ value_high }} as 0.75 (p = 0.75), then your payoff is calculated as - 4 × {{ search_cost }} + {{ value_high }} × 0.75 + {{ value_low }} × (1 - 0.75) = 155.

{% endif %} {% else %}

The value you receive from the item you choose to purchase is either {{ value_high }} or {{ value_low }}.

Suppose that you have searched n times in this round, then your payoff in this round is - n × {{ search_cost }} + {{ value_high }} or - n × {{ search_cost }} + {{ value_low }}.

{% if value_high == 500 %}

Example. Suppose you have searched 4 times and the item you choose to purchase has Probability of Getting {{ value_high }} as 0.75 (p = 0.75), then your payoff could either be - 4 × {{ search_cost }} + {{ value_high }} = 480 points or - 4 × {{ search_cost }} + {{ value_low }} = 80 points.

{% elif value_high == 200 %}

Example. Suppose you have searched 4 times and the item you choose to purchase has Probability of Getting {{ value_high }} as 0.75 (p = 0.75), then your payoff could either be - 4 × {{ search_cost }} + {{ value_high }} = 180 points or - 4 × {{ search_cost }} + {{ value_low }} = 80 points.

{% endif %} {% endif %}
